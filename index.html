Welcome To FISATFocus On Excellence

Welcome to FISAT. Federal Institute of Science And Technology (FISAT)® is a private self financing Engineering College, established and run by the Federal Bank Officers` Association Educational Society (FBOAES). The FBOAES is an initiative of the Federal Bank Officers` Association (FBOA), the sole representative body of the entire officers of the Federal Bank.

Federal Institute of Science And Technology (FISAT) has a unique position in the Professional Education Sector in South India. With the motto "Focus on Excellence", FISAT has been designed and developed to become a `Centre of Excellence` in professional education. Established in the year 2002, the college has carved a niche for itself in education world, eloquently demonstrated by the flying colors attained by its students in academics, placements as well as extra curricular and co curricular activities. FISAT has embarked on an ambitious plan to enhance the quality and value of education and develop high quality individuals. The institution is accredited by NAAC with 'A' Grade. The institution also received the coveted ISO 9001:20015 certification.

Mission and Vision

Our Vision

To become a world class professional institute with focus on excellence, moulding committed global professionals and technocrats who can meet the demands of business, industry and research.

Our Mision

To transform into an advanced centre of technical education, which will, in turn, bring out professionals with superior skills and social commitment.

To provide state-of-the-art facilities to mould brilliant young talents, enabling them to take up challenging assignments in the highly competitive global scenario.

344 offers - 33 companies - 2019 batch
3/03/2019

As the second phase of recruitment of B.Tech, M.Tech and MCA students of the 2019 batch is nearing a close, the number of offers through campus placements have crossed the magic figure of 300. The current recruitment season has been highly successful with major giants like TCS, IBM, Wipro, UST Global all offering considerable number of students.The other major recruiters are Amazon, Federal Bank, IBS, Speridian, DCT, Qburst, Experion, Travancore Analytics, Procsys,Gadgeon, Hexaware, Mitsogo, Poornam, FACE, Kalkitech, Ruby Seven etc.With a few more processes underway, the numbers are expected to cross the all time record of 350 offers.

Third National Conference SECON 19
15/03/2019

Third Edition of the Annual National Conference on Structural Engineering and Construction Management and is organised by Department of Civil Engineering, Federal Institute of Science and Technology. The conference with the theme ‘Sustainable Construction & Design for the future’ along with seven sub themes will express a wide range of issues to be discussed in the conference.The success and appreciation gained in the previous SECON conferences in 2017 and 2018 have been remarkable and we are confident that SECON ’19 will bring significant benefits to the participant post graduate students, researchers, practising engineers, academicians, architects, and developers.
